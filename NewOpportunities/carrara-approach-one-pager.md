# Off-Airport Reporting & Intelligence

**Hertz** | Insurance Replacement, Dealership, Fleet | February 2026

---

## The Problem

Hertz's off-airport division (~35% of P&L) has no single source of truth. Three separate individuals produce reports using different sources, logic, and tools. The numbers rarely reconcile - creating unreliable dashboards, inconsistent partner reporting, and a leadership team that cannot confidently report to C-suite. There is no documentation, no cross-training, and no continuity plan.

## The End State

One source of truth. Every report - internal, partner-facing, and executive - traces back to the same validated baseline. Metrics are clearly defined, pipelines are fully documented, and reporting intelligence is available on demand.

## Benefits

**Reconcilability** - reports always tie out | **Consistency** - one set of definitions everywhere | **Availability** - no dependency on any single person | **Defensibility** - leadership reports with confidence | **Continuity** - knowledge lives in infrastructure, not individuals

## Approach

### Part 1: Trace & Unify

| Step | What We Do | Outcome |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Diagnostics** | Map all workflows, catalogue every report, trace reconciliation gaps to root cause | Clear picture of what exists, where it diverges, and why |
| **Design** | Define single source of truth, agree metric definitions and filtering rules across all segments | Unified blueprint signed off by stakeholders |
| **Rebuild** | Rebuild pipelines against unified model, deliver business-friendly documentation | Clean, reconciled reporting with full traceability |

### Part 2: Agentic Reporting

| Step | What We Do | Outcome |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Design Pipeline** | Define agentic scope and data architecture | Technical blueprint for queryable reporting |
| **Build Pipeline** | Automate scheduled report generation (20+ partner reports, dashboards) | Reports generate without manual intervention |
| **Build Interface** | Deliver real-time query interface by account, segment, location, claim type | On-demand intelligence for any authorised user |

*Hertz may proceed with Part 1, Part 2, or both.*
