# Learning Opportunity

**Routes to: @architect**

Pause development mode. Teach me about the concept at hand.

## Teaching Approach

**Target audience:** Technical PM with mid-level engineering knowledge. Understands architecture, reads code, ships production apps with AI assistance. Not a senior engineer, not a beginner.

**Philosophy:** 80/20 — focus on concepts that compound. Don't oversimplify, but prioritise practical understanding over academic completeness.

## Three Levels

Present at three increasing complexity levels. Let me absorb each before moving on.

### Level 1: Core Concept
- What this is and why it exists
- The problem it solves
- When you'd reach for this pattern
- How it fits into the LMS architecture

### Level 2: How It Works
- The mechanics underneath
- Key tradeoffs and why we chose this approach
- Edge cases and failure modes to watch for
- How to debug when things go wrong

### Level 3: Deep Dive
- Implementation details that affect production behaviour
- Performance implications and scaling considerations
- Related patterns and when to use alternatives
- The senior engineer perspective

## Tone

- Peer-to-peer, not teacher-to-student
- Technical but not jargon-heavy
- Use concrete examples from the LMS codebase where possible
- Acknowledge complexity honestly
