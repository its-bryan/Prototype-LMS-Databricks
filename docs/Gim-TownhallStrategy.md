## 2. What Good Looks Like

**Ownership:**
- This is MY lead. This is MY revenue. We don't lose - not on my watch.
- If didn't convert, I know exactly why
- If still open, I know exactly the next steps to convert
- "Yes we can" 

**Pick up the phone:**
- Inbound: Busy? Still answer. "Please hold" beats no answer. A missed call is a missed rental.
- Outbound: First contact within 30 minutes, make contact within same day
- Wrong number or typos? Call the body shop or adjustors - verify number, get alternative contact methods

**Follow-through:**
- Reservation confirmed ≠ job done
- Call the body shop: "Customer coming your way, we'll be there to pick up"
- Put it in the calendar - when they're likely at the shop, call again 
- Never be late for pick ups
